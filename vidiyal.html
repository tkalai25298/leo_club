        Vidiyal

‘Aezhaiyin Sirippil Iraivanai Kaanbom’ (The Smile of Poor is a medium to reaching God) is a famous saying in Tamil.
Apart from satisfying the educational needs of Suriya Nagar, what if we conduct a cultural event for those downtrodden children?
The result is Vidiyal. charitable mind.

Symbolizing the DAWN (the education) that comes to drive away the darkness (poverty and ignorance), this event rightly provides playful incidents and unforgettable incidents to the children. Categorizing the children into three divisions, based on their class of study, various events are conducted to test their logical reasoning, drawing and writing abilities. There are also absolutely hilarious ones like dance and music to make their day memorable.

  • Can students of CEG participate in Vidiyal?
    No, absolutely not. This is only to make the Suriya Nagar children enjoy a lot and forget their surrounding environment.
  • When will the event be conducted?
    Vidiyal is conducted during the even semester (in our schedule), mostly by February or March.
  • Where will be the events be conducted?
    Vidiyal is conducted entirely in the campus of CEG (mostly in the Red Building). By this way, the children tend to come out of the shell of their locality and enjoy the vast area of our campus. Lunch and refreshments will also be provided for all the incoming participants.
